Client: N&S Franchising Corp ("Nash & Smashed")

This Website Maintenance & Enterprise Local SEO Services Agreement ("Agreement") consolidates and supersedes all prior agreements between the parties, including the Website Development Agreement, Website Maintenance Retainer Addendum, and Enterprise Local SEO System Agreement.


  1. ‌Services Overview

    MarketBrewer LLC will provide the following integrated digital marketing services to support Nash & Smashed's multi-location franchise growth:

    1. ‌Website Maintenance & Development

      • Full React/TypeScript website maintenance for nashandsmashed.com

      • AWS infrastructure management (S3, CloudFront, Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB)

      • Bug fixes, security updates, and performance optimization

      • Content updates and new feature development as needed

      • Form systems maintenance (contact, franchise inquiry, careers)

      • SSL certificate management and DNS configuration


    2. ‌Enterprise Local SEO System

      • Proprietary AI-powered local SEO page generation system

      • Up to 3,000 location-specific landing pages combining target locations with service keywords

      • Interactive Google Maps integration with directions

      • Apple Maps integration for iOS users

      • Location-specific schema markup for enhanced search visibility

      • Dynamic geo-sitemaps for optimal search engine indexing

      • NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all pages

      • Up to 50 target keywords (keywords to be determined collaboratively based on business objectives)


  2. ‌Pricing & Service Details

    1. ‌Monthly Service Fee

      Website Maintenance: $1,000/month

      AI Technology Fee: $250/month (Local SEO page generation)

      Total Monthly Fee: $1,250/month

      This monthly fee includes:

      • Website hosting, updates, and maintenance

      • Up to 3,000 Local SEO pages

      • Up to 50 target keywords

      • Google Search Console + Google Analytics access

      • 1-2 business day response time


  3. ‌Payment Terms

    1. ‌Monthly Fee

      Monthly service fee of $1,250, charged automatically via Stripe subscription on the same date each month.

    2. ‌Late Payment

      • Payments more than 14 days late: Services may be paused

      • Payments more than 30 days late: Services may be suspended

      • Payments more than 60 days late: Agreement may be terminated


  4. ‌Term & Termination

    1. ‌Agreement Term

      This Agreement operates on a month-to-month basis beginning on the Effective Date.


    2. ‌Termination by Either Party

      Either party may terminate with 45 days written notice to the other party.


    3. ‌Effect of Termination

      Upon termination:

      1. All maintenance, updates, support, and hosting by Provider will cease at the end of the notice period

      2. Client retains ownership of their domain and all brand content (photos, menus, copy, business/location info)

      3. Provider will supply a complete export of Client-owned content upon request

      4. Client retains admin access to all third-party accounts (Google Analytics, Search Console, Tag Manager, etc.)

      5. Client is free to engage another service provider

      6. No refunds for partial months


    4. ‌Extended Hosting Option

      If Client requires additional time to transition after the notice period, Provider will continue hosting the website for $200/month until cutover is complete.


  5. ‌Client Responsibilities

    1. Provide accurate business information for all locations

    2. Review and approve content within 5 business days of submission

    3. Ensure legal compliance of all approved content

    4. Maintain current contact and billing information

    5. Designate primary contact for coordination

    6. Ensure franchise brand guideline compliance

    7. Notify Provider of new locations within 4 days of opening


  6. ‌Franchise Compliance

    Given Client's status as a franchisor:

    • All content will adhere to Nash & Smashed franchise brand guidelines

    • Client retains final approval authority over all published content

    • Content modifications available upon franchisor requirement changes


  7. ‌Liability & Legal Terms

    1. ‌Liability Cap

      Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Client during the twelve (12) months preceding any claim.

    2. ‌No SEO Guarantees

      Search engine rankings depend on factors beyond Provider's control. Provider provides best-effort optimization but cannot guarantee specific ranking positions.

    3. ‌Governing Law

      This Agreement is governed by Virginia law. Any disputes shall be resolved in Fairfax County, Virginia courts.
